## Final Clean Dataset for Coding Challenge

This dataset contains the total HAM score, mean HAM score, latest HAM score, 
and HAM score closest to one year after first consent for participants of 
interest.    
  
A data frame with 90 observations on the following 8 variables.  
  
new_ID (character)  
: ID of participant
  
total_ham_score (numeric)  
: The sum of the participant's HAM scores across every visit
  
mean_ham_score (numeric)  
: The participant's mean HAM score
  
latest_ham_score (numeric)  
: The participant's most recent HAM score
  
latest_ham_date (Date)  
: The date the participant's most recent HAM was administered
  
first_consent (Date)  
: The participant's first consent date
  
year_after_ham_date (Date)  
: The date closest to one year after the participant's first consent date when they had a HAM administered
  
year_after_ham_score (numeric)  
: The participant's HAM score closest to one year after their first consent
